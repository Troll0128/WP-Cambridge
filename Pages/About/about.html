Who am I?



My name is Pablo Uribe Zuluaga, I study in ASPAEN Gimnasio horizontes from Manizales, currently coursing Tenth Grade.

When I was about 10 years old, I got interested in a special board my dad keeped in a drawer, so I decided to ask him what was that, he told me that was a chess board and he showed me the pieces, then he asked me if I wanted to learn how to play, obviously I accept. He teached me moves and the obvious objective of the game which is give checkmate to the opponent, then we started to play and he won because he played well. Then I look for people at school who played with me, at first, they won, but with the ability, experience and practice, I finish winning.
